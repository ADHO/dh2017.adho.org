Accommodations

The organizers have negotiated special rates with three hotels located near the conference, as well as dorm rooms on McGill campus.

Delta Hotels Montreal (475 President-Kennedy Avenue, Montreal, QC H3A 1J7) — CDN$299/night

Le Centre Sheraton Montreal Hotel (1201 Boulevard Rene-Levesque West, Montreal, QC H3B 2L7) — CDN$249/night

Hotel Espresso Montreal Downtown (1005 rue Guy, Montreal, Qc, H3H 2K4) — CDN$199/night [Please call the hotel @ 514-938-4611 for booking with the group rate ADHO]

Dorm rooms on McGill campus

The Royal Victoria College  (3425 University Street, Montreal, Qc, H3A 2A8) – – CDN$45.00/night

The Royal Victoria College offers traditional residence accommodations. Each room is equipped with a small fridge, access to shared bathrooms (multiple private toilets and shower stalls) and common kitchen. All rooms are supplied with fresh linen, towels and soap. A/C not available, fans can be rented ($). In the study room on the main floor we provide computers, as well in-room free WI-FI available.

1 Single bed – – Limited on-site parking is CDN$20/day

New Residence Hall (3625 Parc Avenue, Montreal, Qc, H2X 3P8) – – CDN$124.00/night

New Residence Hall offers hotel type accommodations. Each room is equipped with a private washroom, A/C, cable television, standard amenities, free-local calls and WI-FI; plus daily housekeeping and a Business Center as a complimentary service. Breakfast included.

1 or 2 double-sized beds – – Underground parking (one in & out access) is CDN$24/day.

Carrefour Sherbrooke (475 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, Qc, H3A 2L9) – – CDN$115.00/night

Carrefour Sherbrooke offers hotel type accommodations. Each room is equipped with a private washroom, A/C, cable television, standard amenities, free-local calls and WI-FI; plus daily housekeeping and a Fitness Center available free of charge. Breakfast included.

1 or 2 queen-sized beds – – Off-site parking is CDN$25/day.

La Citadelle  (410 Sherbrooke West Street, Montreal, Qc, H3A 1B3) – – CDN$124.00/night

La Citadelle is the newest hotel type accommodation. Each room is equipped with a private washroom, A/C, cable television, free local calls and WI-FI; plus daily housekeeping and both Fitness and Business Center available free of charge.

1 queen-sized bed or 2 double-sized beds – – Off-site parking is CDN$25/day.
